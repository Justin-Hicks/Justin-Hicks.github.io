- About -

I'm Justin Hicks and this is some of the work I'm proud of. Maybe you're here because you're thinking of hiring me for a job. Or, maybe you're my mom wondering what I've been up to. (Hi, Mom!) Either way, thanks for coming. (Yes, Mom, I'll call more often.)

I'm passionate about telling important stories at the intersection of people and policies that, hopefully, help create change. I've worked and lived all over the country from New York City to Los Angeles and everywhere between. I'm currently a public radio reporter, so radio reporting is my meat and potatoes. However, I also enjoy photography and videography -- which, as you'll see, bleed over into my work as well. All the content you see on this site (and some of the coding, too!) was created by yours truly.
